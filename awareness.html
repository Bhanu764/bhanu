Awareness

Good sanitation prevents disease and keeps our communities safe. Please keep your surroundings clean, dispose of waste properly, and report issues like blocked drains, overflowing bins, or stagnant water.

  • Hand hygiene: Wash hands with soap regularly.
  • Waste disposal: Use proper bins, segregate wet/dry waste.
  • Water safety: Avoid stagnant water; it attracts mosquitoes.
  • Community action: Report problems early to prevent outbreaks.
